Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Yeast
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Mix together flour, water, salt, and yeast
  2. Kneed
  3. Allow dough to rise
  4. Roll out
  5. Add sauce, pepperoni, and mozzarella
  6. bake in oven for 20 minutes at 350 degrees
